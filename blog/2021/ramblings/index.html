Ramblings of a University Student

January 3, 2021

Reading blogs posted by other people is a fun way to pass time. On the other hand, writing blogs is not. I started this blog because I liked the idea of writing down my experiences, and also because it would be cool for me to look back on. I also tried to tailor my posts towards an (imaginary) audience, but it’s mostly just for myself. I think my blog post are of decent length, but, in truth, I only wanted to write down my passing thoughts; most of them don’t really need a whole post. I really only wanted a space to jot down some memories… which is what this blog post will be dedicated to! I’m going to use this post to write short paragraphs of what I’m thinking. It’s like a diary, but worse.

1/3/2021 Gun Point

A few years ago, I had an acquaintance tell me, “I don’t understand why people in movies wet their pants when held at gun point. I don’t think I’d be scared even if someone pointed their gun at me.” At the time, I thought, wow, what a stupid thought. Even now, I still think that was a stupid thought. It probably highlights a problem with desensitization towards violence or something of that sort. But what I’m more curious about is how this person would react if a close one passed away. It’s one thing to not fear a gun, but to disregard your own life probably means they don’t understand the fragility of their own lives and the lives around them.
